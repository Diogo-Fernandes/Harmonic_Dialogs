Teaser

An empty canvas and a mind full of thoughts.

Harmonic Dialogs is a Computer Vision system intertwined with an empty canvas, so the users may draw and express any static or moving images that he wants

We intend to create an experience for all those who wish to stimulate their creativity, imagination, and personal expression by exploring non-traditional communication concepts. The range of possibilities are endless. Through this fresh way of communicating, users could get an "Out of the Box" experience, having fun with other users while also leaving their mark during the experience so others may see.

Benchmarking

Allowing users to enjoy a playful experience without creativity limits, giving rise to the imagination, is the only rule. The first concepts the group thought about was to make a project that could bring educational benefits to stimulate attention and interaction among participants. We explore the different ways of communicating and the importance of communication and personal expression to develop personal skills. Taking advantage of the Double Diamond process, the group first established the target audience, which range from teenagers to young adults, and later on outlined later steps such as the "Why" and the "How" of this project.
Hence HARMONIC DIALOGS is born, a social interaction experience of exploring and expressing one's feelings, while also re-living drawings from past users.

Inspirations

Augmented Reality Sandbox

The Augmented Reality Sandbox, made by the UCLA, allows students and the public to interact with a miniature landscape, sculpting mountains, valleys, rivers and even volcanoes, with off the shelf readily available parts.

Link to the demonstration

Messa Di Voce

Messa Di Voce, by Golan Levin, augments the speech, shouts and songs produced by a pair of virtuoso vocalists with real-time interactive visualizations. Custom software transforms every vocal nuance into correspondingly complex, subtly differentiated and highly expressive graphics, enabling a highly-expressive environment.
